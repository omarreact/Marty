The Marty Waite Experience

Who is
Marty Waite?
Gatineau Real Estate Agent (Aylmer, Hull, Buckingham, Masson, Chelsea, Pontiac)

Since 2004, I have had the privilege of working as a real estate broker in the Gatineau-Ottawa region. Founding President of L’Experience Marty Waite since 2007, I consider myself to be a rigorous businessman for more than 20 years.

I am much more than an accomplished manager and a respected real estate influencer. I am a force that shapes industry, brings innovation, excellence and passion to every stage of my career.

My name has become synonymous with success in the real estate sector, and I am proud to lead the number one team at Remax Québec in 2021 in terms of transactions. But what really distinguishes me is my authentic and remarkable personality. The community around me recognizes my quality as an optimistic, benevolent and elegant professional, a true gentleman. These precious traits are rooted in me through my family’s legacy, and they guide my way of honouring the invaluable education I received from my parents.

However, I can only attribute my success to the incredible teamwork to which I am committed every day. My pride lies in my team of outstanding brokers, loyal clients and distinguished coaches. Together, in a philosophy of work and life imbued with positivity, we renew our achievements week after week, year after year.

My dedication and passion have also been rewarded nationally. In addition, as the number one team at Remax Direct since 2011, I have dominated the Outaouais real estate market for about 20 years.

My career is much more than just a professional success. It is a story of perseverance, hard work and deep-rooted values. I am determined to continue to inspire and push the boundaries of real estate, while remaining faithful to my fundamental principles.
